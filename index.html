Project NeoCloud

An AI-powered digital human creation and content generation Agent SaaS platform for education, healthcare, and live-streaming.

Vision

To build a globally leading AI-driven digital human platform that makes content creation in education, healthcare, and live-streaming more efficient and interactive, allowing everyone to easily create intelligent digital personas.

Mission

By offering easy-to-use SaaS tools, help organizations and individuals quickly generate intelligent digital humans, improve content production efficiency and user experience, and drive intelligent transformation across industries.

Development Timeline

  • 2025 Q1: Core framework design and MVP construction
  • 2025 Q2: Education scenario feature iteration and testing
  • 2025 Q3: Healthcare scenario integration and security review
  • 2025 Q4: Live-streaming scenario optimization and public beta release
  • 2026 Q1: Full platform commercial deployment and user outreach
